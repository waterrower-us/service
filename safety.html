Safety Precautions




Important WaterRower Safety Guidelines

Thank you for purchasing a WaterRower. Please read below a few important safety guidelines to follow while storing or using your WaterRower.

I. Please consult a physician before commencing any exercise program.

II. Wear proper exercise clothing when rowing.

III. Injury may occur if equipment is used improperly. Please refer to www.howtorow.com for advice on how to properly use the WaterRower, rowing technique, and training advice.

IV. To protect against injury, please ensure others do not touch machine when in use. Areas of concern are highlighted in the images below.

V. Keep all observers, especially children and pets away from moving seat wheels, straps and pulleys.

VI. Place the handle back into the handle rest after use. Do not let go of the handle when rowing.

VII. When the WaterRower is in its upright storage position, take care it is not bumped which may cause it to fall over. Take extra caution with children.

VIII. Do not pull on the handle when the WaterRower is upright. This can cause the WaterRower to fall over.

By following these precautions and using common sense, you will have many safe and pleasurable years of healthful exercise on your WaterRower.
